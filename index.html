The main coffee trends in 2023

Recently in the coffee market, you can notice several interesting trends that have become popular among lovers of this drink. One of these trends is an increase in demand for micro-plantation coffee, which guarantees high quality and great taste.

How to choose coffee for yourself?

Coffee is not just a drink, it is a whole world with its own intricacies and peculiarities. There are various factors to consider when choosing coffee, such as taste preferences, type of caffeine, brewing method, and more..

Coffee in the culture of different countries

In Italy, coffee is not just a drink, but almost a national treasure. Italian bars make coffee so delicious that it is there that you have to queue. Coffee in Italy is not just a small cup of espresso, but also cappuccino, ristretto, latte, macchiato, etc.
